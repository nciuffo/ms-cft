Overview

We are responsible for the technology behind many critical functions and businesses across Morgan Stanley

Corporate

It takes a force to run an organization with more than 176 offices and nearly 56,000 people. The firm’s corporate functions are that force, and we provide technology and solutions that help them remain efficient and effective.

Funding

Our technology solutions help connect all activities required to fund the Firm – stock loan, secured funding, treasury and cash management. Our focus is to optimize Morgan Stanley’s capital, funding and liquidity profile.

Business

We support equity financing activities within the Firm’s Institutional Securities Group. Our platforms provide best-in-class technology and service to Morgan Stanley’s hedge fund and high-net-worth clients in Asia.
